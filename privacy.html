Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

AclTime Pro is an Android application developed for educational, scientific, and exploratory purposes. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when using the application.

1. Information Collection

AclTime Pro does not require user account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information by default.

Any data entered by the user (such as body parameters or time-related information) is used solely for local calculations within the application.

2. Data Usage

User-provided data is used exclusively for:

  • Alcohol elimination time estimation
  • Simulation and analytical comparison
  • Educational and exploratory purposes

No data is used for medical diagnosis, legal assessment, or decision-making support.

3. Data Storage

All data processing occurs locally on the user's device unless explicitly stated otherwise. AclTime Pro does not transmit personal data to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

AclTime Pro does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are applied to ensure local data integrity. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

AclTime Pro is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. No data is knowingly collected from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the project evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
